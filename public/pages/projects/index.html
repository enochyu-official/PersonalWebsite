Problem of the Day
(100 Problems for 100 Days)

As the name suggests, the project contains 100 problems from diverse sources, where I made a video for the problems. The playlist to the explanation videos are available here! The solutions are mixed works of my personal solutions, solutions founded by community, and official solutions.
I have started this project to motivate myself and share my progress for the 2025 Korean Mathematical Olympiad Stage II.
